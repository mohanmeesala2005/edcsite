About Us

ABOUT US

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell, NIT Durgapur is the institution’s official Entrepreneurship Promoting student body. Through a plethora of initiatives throughout the year, we remain steadfast to our sole dream of creating a sustainable and diverse entrepreneurial ecosystem where the young and the elderly come together to make entrepreneurship a common reality in India.

Innovation and Incubation Council

Innovation and Incubation Council, NIT Durgapur is NIT Durgapur’s very own incubation body, established under the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India. EDC, NIT Durgapur is an integral part of IIIC, NIT Durgapur and works together with the faculty members of IIIC to carry out various entrepreneurial activities and projects in the campus.

Our Events

EDC’s numerous events enjoys overwhelming participation from students in NIT Durgapur, and across the country. Some of our flagship events are: E-SUMMIT:The second largest E-Summit in eastern India, graced by the biggest entrepreneurs and celebrity speakers. BizzCup: A fun event designed exclusively for the freshmen students. Pitchfest: A nationwide B-Plan Competition, with huge funding and mentorship opportunities for winning proposals. Hult Prize: On Campus Round of the renowned international competition, Hult Prize organised every year by EDC NIT Durgapur and the list goes on.

Our Journey

In 2015, inspired by a desire to instil an entrepreneurial culture in NIT Durgapur, a group of three friends decided to start the first independent Entrepreneurship Cell of the College. What began as a small endeavour by three young minds has today risen to stand tall with over 70 members, achieving unimaginable things in just a span of few years. This journey would have never been possible without EDC’s incredible alumni who have worked ever so tirelessly to take the clubs to the height it has scaled. Today, EDC proudly figures among the most prolific entrepreneurship cells of the country, and we only hope to get bigger and better in the years to come!

OUR STARTUPS

PedalsGo is mainly working on cycle rental where booking is done via call. This is basically a campus connect programme where the cycle rental facilities are provided to the campus residents. It is a platform which is integrating many cycles under one umbrella. In future, PedalsGo will work automatically, where booking will be done through an app. Not only cycles but also electric scooters will be provided in future. The vehicles will be tracked through their entire journey.
